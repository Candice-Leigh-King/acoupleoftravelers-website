About Us

Hi, we are Lo & Jay! Travel enthusiasts- based out of Texas. We are 25 years young with a passion for seeing the world and learning about new places.

Lo has dreamed of seeing the world since she was a little girl. Her favorite subjects in school were Anthropology, Archaeology, History, and World Geography.

Jay is new to the travel community. After meeting Lo in 2016, he joined her on his FIRST EVER flight to Iceland, and his life was changed. He realized how much there was to see and experiene in this world, and it has become a shared priority for the couple.

" The world is too big to just tell one story."


Follow us on our journey around the world.
