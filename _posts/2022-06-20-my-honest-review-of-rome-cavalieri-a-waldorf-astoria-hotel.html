When I tell you this place is my favorite hotel I’ve ever been to, I MEAN IT! Here’s why-

Luxurious is an understatement! This hotel is a perfect getaway for those that want luxury but don’t want to pay thousands of dollars. (If you’re a Hilton honors member, you can book on points! Click here to sign up for their credit card & earn points easily. Not financial advice. Please talk with a financial advisor.)

Their breakfast is *chefs kiss* and I’m addicted to their eggs. I dream of them. (Complimentary when staying there too!)

They have a 3 Michelin Star restaurant on site called La Pergola! We haven’t had a chance to dine here unfortunately, so make sure you get reservations!!! Don’t be like us and miss out.

The spa is beautiful with an indoor pool, saunas, massages, and more! We loved going in the evening when you can see how dark it is outside & the indoor pool is glowing.

The rooms are so unique. They definitely give off the Italian vibe! Check out a tour here!

The customer service is impeccable! They really do go above and beyond. We have stayed here multiple times, and one time when we stayed, they forgot our complimentary water. Later, they surprised us with not only a lot more water, but gourmet chocolates! How sweet is that!!

Now, I know what you’re thinking. This sounds too good to be true. Where is it located? Can I walk everywhere? Well- it is outside of the city center, HOWEVER, they have a complimentary shuttle that drops you off about a 10 min walk to the Trevi fountain! It picks up & drops off in the same spot every day at the same times, and the front desk will provide you a guide with a map & times the shuttle is going. We found it very efficient & worked perfectly.
