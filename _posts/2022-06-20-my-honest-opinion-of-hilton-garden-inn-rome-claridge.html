If you’re looking for a hotel that’s somewhere between bougie and on a budget, this one is for you!

Located a 5 min walk away from Villa Borghese is Hilton Garden Inn Rome Claridge! If you are a Hilton honors member, use your points! We booked this hotel using 30,000 points per night! What a steal! (Click this link to apply for the Hilton Honors Amex card & earn points on every day spending! *Not financial advice. Please do your own research and talk to a financial advisor with questions.*)

The staff is very welcoming, and on our second trip to Rome, they set us up with a private shuttle to pick us up from the airport! They made it very stress free, especially arriving after a red eye flight!

We have stayed here twice & had a fabulous stay each time! (Click here for our room/hotel tour) we stayed in this room the first time we visited, and stayed in a smaller room the second time! ** TIP if you come during off season be sure to ask up front if the air conditioning is running.**

Complimentary breakfast is self serve buffet style. They had typical Italian staples like cappuccinos and pastries, and typical American staples like eggs, waffles, and bacon. They have other options, as well! It’s a great way to start the day, so you can fuel yourself for your day of adventures!

There is an on-site bar that serves aperitivo, which is a must in Italy. They offer room service if you’re too tired from walking all day! However, the location is right next to 3 of my favorite restaurants in Rome, so I would definitely walk l Caffe Hungaria, Mattarello Pariloi, or Taverna Rossini. To see my fav restaurants in Rome, click here!

One thing I enjoyed about this hotel is it’s not all tourist. There were other locals having breakfast there, having meetings, and the restaurants nearby had locals, and not every one working spoke English. That’s how you know the food is going to be fabulous!

They do have a gym on site if that’s important to you! When in Rome you’ll do a lot of walking, but if you need some weight lifting in your life, they got you covered!

There are many options  for hotels throughout Rome, but this one is definitely worth checking out!
