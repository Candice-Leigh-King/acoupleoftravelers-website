Best places to eat in Reykjavík!

For all my foodies out there, this one’s for you! Now that you’ve read my 3 day Reykjavík, Iceland itinerary it’s time to discuss where to eat in Iceland!

I’m going to make this short & simple:

Bakeries/Ice Cream:

  • Brauð & Co.
  • Valdis

Cafes:

  • The Coocoo's Nest
  • Bergsson Mathús
  • Sandholt

Restaurants:

  • La Primavera Ristorante
  • Bryggjan Brugghús
  • Messinn
  • Þrír Frakkar
  • Skál!
  • Reykjavík Chips

  • Bæjarins Beztu Pylsur
