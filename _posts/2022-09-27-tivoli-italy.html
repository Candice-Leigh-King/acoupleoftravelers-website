When people dream of European Travel, a lot think of Italy as their first destination, and rightfully so! It is a charming country, full of romance and history.

Italy is one of my favorite places to travel too. You can find me sitting on the steps of Ponte Sisto Fountain, enjoying live street music in the Trastevere, and eating as much authentic Italian food as I can handle.

When I’m in Rome, I love to visit Tivoli. It is a small city less than an hour outside of Rome. Many tours go here, or you can venture on your own. I recommend a tour with a guide for your first time, and you’ll definitely want to visit again. This is the tour we did: Tivoli Tour. Our guide was very knowledgable. Tivoli also has a lot of cute shops, trattoria’s, and kind locals.

Tivoli is home to Hadrian’s Villa & Villa d’Este.  Hadrian’s Villa is a UNESCO World Heritage Site comprising the ruins and archaeological remains built c. AD 120 by Roman Emperor Hadrian in Tivoli outside Rome. Villa d’Este is a symbol of the Italian Renaissance, an Italian State Museum, and UNESCO World Heritage Site! Both of these are a site to see. I highly recommend visiting Tivoli if you have time.

10 Best Things to Do in Tivoli (Italy):

  • Villa Adriana
  • Villa d’Este Gardens
  • Villa d’Este Palace
  • Rocca Pia
  • Tivoli Cathedral
  • Villa Gregoriana
  • Chiesa di Sant’Andrea
  • Tempio di Vesta
  • Piazza Giuseppe Garibaldi
  • Enjoy a meal at a local trattoria
