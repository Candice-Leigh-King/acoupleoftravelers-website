10 Best Things to Do in Rome (Italy):

 

  • Take a cooking class! Learn to make classic Italian dishes!

  • Visit the Colosseum! Skip the Line: Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill Tour: Head into the Colosseum to walk in the footsteps of gladiators! Stroll amid the ruins of the Roman Forum, then discover layers of Roman history on Palatine Hill.

  • Visit the Vatican! Skip-the-Line Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel Group Tour: Discover the sacred and artistic treasures of the Vatican

  • Roman Catacombs! Skip the Line: Crypts and Roman Catacombs Small-Group Guided Tour: Go underground to see Rome’s ancient catacombs and crypts on a half-day tour.

  • Do a food tour in the Trastevere! 

  • Have Gelato at the Trevi Fountain. Such an instagramable moment! Be sure to get there early!

  • The Pantheon! Entrance is free, but lines start early.

  • Drink aperol spritz and walk the city! Check out my 3-day Rome Itinerary here!

  • Eat “street food”! My favorite is Supplì!

  • Take a day trip! Check out my 3 favorite day-trips here!
